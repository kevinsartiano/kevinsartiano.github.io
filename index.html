Projects

  • Jira-Slack Notification Bot

    This app combines the Jira API and the Slack API to send custom notifications any time a ticket is assigned to you or your team. I developed this bot to solve an issue that my team had keeping up to date with Jira alerts. It received great feedback and proved itself a big success.




    Jira-Slack Notification Bot

  • Airline Scraper

    My thesis in Computer Engineering aims at finding out whether airlines apply price discrimination when selling tickets. To collect the data, I designed and built a flexible scraper using Python, Selenium and the Amadeus APIs. The research will be published in a scientific journal.




    Airline Scraper

  • Toggl Helper MacOS App

    The goal of this app is to offer an easier a more efficient way of tracking working time in Toggl. This app makes use of the package Rumps for the macOS GUI. The data entered in the window is stored in a spreadsheet file and exported to the web platform through the Toggl API.




    Toggl Helper

  • Germany COVID-19 Tracker

    This is a web application that tracks the 7-day cases per 100k inhabitants in Germany using the data sourced from the Robert Koch Institute API. The web application was written using Python, Flask and Folium and it is deployed using Heroku. You can find it here.




    Germany COVID-19 Tracker

  • German Verbs Android App

    German is hard, but not impossible to learn. To boost my mobile development skills, I created an Android app using Python and Kivy. The app was specifically crafted to help my fellow VHS students (and myself 😅) in learning verb conjugations for upcoming exams.




    German Verbs Android App

Born and raised in La Spezia in Italy, from an early age I developed a keen interest in technology which I cultivated throughout the years. As a teenager, I had a strong desire to see the world, so I took part in an exchange student program and spent the senior year of high school in the USA, developing my English skills and falling in love with American Football. After obtaining my high school degree, I joined the family business in the hospitality sector.

In 2013, I was offered to travel the world by joining the Formula 1 circus, catering the Sahara Force India and Honda teams. This gave me the opportunity to gain international experience and explore new cultures. During a stay in France, I first met my fiancée who was on her semester abroad from Germany.

In 2017, I moved to Germany to be with her and soon after I decided to follow my passion for IT and become a software developer. To pay for my Computer Engineering studies, I worked as a Sales Agent at Flyline where I distinguished myself for my technical and interpersonal skills which led me to represent my team at international sales meetings.

In 2019, once I gained enough knowledge in software development, I joined Arriver where I supported the Software Release Management team in delivering the final software product to the customers. In 2021, after obtaining a bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering with a 95% final grade (104/110 Italian scale, 1.4 German scale), I joined agnosco.net as a Junior Content Manager.
